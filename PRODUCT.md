# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Urban dwellers, young adults, college students, and newcomers looking to discover real-time events, local community activity, and spontaneous social connections in their city.

## Product Purpose
Yuno transforms the static map-based social network into a live "City Intelligence" layer. It fetches real-time local events and updates from Reddit, geocodes and highlights them as glowing interactive pins, and helps users match and meet up through AI wingman chat starters and automated group chats.

## Brand Personality
Intriguing discovery, warm local warmth, urban exploration. Sophisticated neutral colors, rich leather accents, pulsing glowing highlights, crisp clean typography.

## Anti-references
- SaaS-cream / paper-neutral monotone layouts (the typical 2026 AI default).
- Massive, over-saturated rainbow gradients.
- Generic grid of cards containing lucide icons.
- Tracked uppercase eyebrows above every heading.

## Design Principles
- **Elevated Contrast & Identity**: Avoid light gray typography on beige backgrounds. Use high contrast, ink-colored text.
- **Geospatial Focus**: Keep the map and immediate surroundings as the visual centerpiece.
- **Clean Rhythm**: Space out components using strict, harmonious padding and margin variables.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
WCAG AA compliance. High contrast ratios (4.5:1 minimum), clear focus indicators, supports prefers-reduced-motion with instant transitions or clean fades.
